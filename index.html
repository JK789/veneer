Sustainable Sourced Wood Veneer made in the USA

Sustainably Sourced

All of our wood is sustainably sourced. We specialize in wood species in our botanical range to reduce the carbon footprint.
Our wood is sourced in the United States. We use wood that was destined to be thrown away, cut out its imperfections and then turn it into beautiful, usable veneer for a variety of crafts.
All of our wood is manufactured, packaged and shipped right here in the Pacific Northwest made by our team in America.
